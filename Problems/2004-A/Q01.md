# AMC12 2004-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2004-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Alicia earns $\$20$ per hour, of which $1.45\%$ is deducted to pay local taxes. How many cents per hour of Alicia's wages are used to pay local taxes?

(A) $0.0029$
(B) $0.029$
(C) $0.29$
(D) $2.9$
(E) $29$

## Answer

**E.** $29$

## Solution

Since $\$20$ is $2000$ cents, the tax is
$$
 0.0145 \times 2000 = 29
$$
 cents per hour.

Thus, the correct answer is **E**.

## Knowledge points

- Percentage
- Unit conversion

## Key idea

Translate the percentage into an equation for the same total.

## Common mistakes

- Do not use a formula before identifying exactly which quantities the statement fixes.
- Keep track of units, signs, and endpoint conditions throughout the calculation.
