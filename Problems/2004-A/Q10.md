# AMC12 2004-A Q10

![[assets/problems/2004-A/Q10.png]]

## Problem

The sum of $49$ consecutive integers is $7^5.$ What is their median?

(A) $7$
(B) $7^2$
(C) $7^3$
(D) $7^4$
(E) $7^5$

## Answer

**C.** $7^3$

## Solution

The sum of a set of consecutive integers equals the number of terms times their mean, and for consecutive integers the mean equals the median.

So the median is
$$
 \dfrac{7^5}{49} = \dfrac{7^5}{7^2} = 7^3 = 343.
$$

Thus, the correct answer is **C**.

- Mean

## Key idea

Use the constant difference and the formula for partial sums.
