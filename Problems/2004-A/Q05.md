# AMC12 2004-A Q05

![[assets/problems/2004-A/Q05.png]]

## Problem

The graph of a line $y = mx + b$ is shown. Which of the following is true?

(A) $mb \lt -1$
(B) $-1 \lt mb \lt 0$
(C) $mb = 0$
(D) $0 \lt mb \lt 1$
(E) $mb \gt 1$

## Answer

**B.** $-1 \lt mb \lt 0$

## Solution

The $y$-intercept of the line is between $0$ and $1,$ so $0 \lt b \lt 1.$

The slope is negative and shallow, between $-1$ and $0,$ so $-1 \lt m \lt 0.$

The product $mb$ is therefore negative with absolute value less than $1,$ giving $-1 \lt mb \lt 0.$

Thus, the correct answer is **B**.

## Knowledge points

- Slope
- Coordinate geometry

## Key idea

Use the slope formula to turn the geometry into equations.

## Common mistakes

- Do not use a formula before identifying exactly which quantities the statement fixes.
- Keep track of units, signs, and endpoint conditions throughout the calculation.
