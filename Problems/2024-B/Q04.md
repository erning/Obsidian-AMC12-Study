# AMC12 2024-B Q04

![[assets/problems/2024-B/Q04.png]]

## Problem

Balls numbered $1,2,3,\ldots$ are deposited into five bins $A,B,C,D,E$. Step $1$ puts one ball in $A$, step $2$ puts two balls in $B$, step $3$ puts three balls in $C$, and so on, with the bins cycling after $E$. In which bin is ball $2024$ deposited?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(D) $D$**

## Solution

After step $k$, the number of balls deposited is

$$
1+2+\cdots+k=\frac{k(k+1)}2.
$$

Since

$$
\frac{63\cdot64}{2}=2016
$$

and

$$
\frac{64\cdot65}{2}=2080,
$$

ball $2024$ is deposited during step $64$.

The bins used by steps $1,2,3,4,5$ are $A,B,C,D,E$, respectively. Since

$$
64\equiv4\pmod5,
$$

step $64$ uses bin $D$.

## Knowledge points

- Triangular numbers
- Modular cycles
- Interval localization

## Key idea

Locate the step using triangular numbers, then reduce the step number modulo $5$.

## Common mistakes

- Using $k(k+1)$ without dividing by $2$.
- Assigning step $5$ to $A$ instead of $E$.
