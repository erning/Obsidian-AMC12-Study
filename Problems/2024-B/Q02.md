# AMC12 2024-B Q02

![[assets/problems/2024-B/Q02.png]]

## Problem

What is

$$
10!-7!\cdot6!?
$$

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(B) $0$**

## Solution

Observe that

$$
6!=720=8\cdot9\cdot10.
$$

Therefore

$$
7!\cdot6!
=7!\cdot8\cdot9\cdot10
=10!.
$$

The difference is

$$
10!-7!\cdot6!=\boxed0.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Factorial expansion
- Recognizing consecutive factors

## Key idea

Rewrite $10!=7!\cdot8\cdot9\cdot10$.

## Common mistakes

- Treating $7!\cdot6!$ as $13!$.
- Calculating huge factorials unnecessarily.
