# AMC12 2005-A Q05

![[assets/problems/2005-A/Q05.png]]

## Problem

The average (mean) of $20$ numbers is $30,$ and the average of $30$ other numbers is $20.$ What is the average of all $50$ numbers?

(A) $23$
(B) $24$
(C) $25$
(D) $26$
(E) $27$

## Answer

**B.** $24$

## Solution

The total of all $50$ numbers is $20 \cdot 30 + 30 \cdot 20$ $= 600 + 600$ $= 1200.$

The average is $1200 \div 50 = 24.$

Thus, the correct answer is **B**.

## Knowledge points

- Mean

## Key idea

Convert the mean into an equation for the total sum.
