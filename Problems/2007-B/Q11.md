# AMC12 2007-B Q11

![[assets/problems/2007-B/Q11.png]]

## Problem

The angles of quadrilateral $ABCD$ satisfy $\angle A=2\angle B=3\angle C=4\angle D.$ What is the degree measure of $\angle A,$ rounded to the nearest whole number?

(A) $125$
(B) $144$
(C) $153$
(D) $173$
(E) $180$

## Answer

**D.** $173$

## Solution

Let $x=\angle A.$ Then $\angle B=\tfrac x2,$ $\angle C=\tfrac x3,$ and $\angle D=\tfrac x4.$

The angle sum gives
$$
 x+\dfrac x2+\dfrac x3+\dfrac x4=\dfrac{25x}{12}=360,
$$
 so $x=\dfrac{12\cdot360}{25}=172.8\approx173.$

Thus, the correct answer is **D**.

## Knowledge points

- Angle sum
- Ratio and proportion

## Key idea

Use the sum of angles in the relevant triangle or polygon.
