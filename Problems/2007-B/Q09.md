![[assets/problems/2007-B/Q09.png]]

## Problem

A function $f$ has the property that $f(3x-1)=x^2+x+1$ for all real numbers $x.$ What is $f(5)?$

(A) $7$
(B) $13$
(C) $31$
(D) $111$
(E) $211$

## Answer

**A.** $7$

## Solution

Setting $3x-1=5$ gives $x=2.$

Then
$$
 f(5)=2^2+2+1=7.
$$

Thus, the correct answer is **A**.
