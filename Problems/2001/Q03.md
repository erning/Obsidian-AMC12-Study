# AMC12 2001 Q03

![[assets/problems/2001/Q03.png]]

## Problem

The state income tax where Kristin lives is levied at the rate of $p\%$ of the first $\$28000$ of annual income plus $(p + 2)\%$ of any amount above $\$28000.$ Kristin noticed that the state income tax she paid amounted to $(p + 0.25)\%$ of her annual income. What was her annual income?

(A) $\$28000$
(B) $\$32000$
(C) $\$35000$
(D) $\$42000$
(E) $\$56000$

## Answer

**B.** $\$32000$

## Solution

Let her income be $x \gt 28000$ dollars. Writing the tax with both descriptions and multiplying by $100,$
$$
 \begin{aligned} &p \cdot 28000 \\ &\quad {}+ (p + 2)(x - 28000) \\ &= (p + 0.25)x. \end{aligned}
$$

Expanding, every term containing $p$ cancels, leaving
$$
 2x - 56000 = 0.25x,
$$
 so $1.75x = 56000$ and $x = 32000.$

Thus, the correct answer is **B**.

## Key idea

- Let her income be $x$ dollars with $x \gt 28000,$ and write the tax two different ways
- Set $p \cdot 28000$ $+ (p + 2)(x - 28000)$ $= (p + 0.25)x$; the $p$ terms cancel
