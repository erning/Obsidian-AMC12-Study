# AMC12 2014-B Q03

![[assets/problems/2014-B/Q03.png]]

## Problem

Randy drove the first third of his trip on gravel, the next $20$ miles on pavement, and the remaining one-fifth on dirt. How long was the trip?

## Answer

**E.** $\frac{300}{7}$ miles

## Solution

The pavement portion is
$$1-\frac13-\frac15=\frac7{15}$$
of the trip. Therefore the total distance $T$ satisfies
$$\frac7{15}T=20,\qquad T=\frac{300}{7}.$$

## Knowledge points

- Fractions of a whole
- Proportional reasoning

## Key idea

Find the fraction represented by the known $20$ miles.

## Common mistakes

- Adding the first and last fractions instead of subtracting from $1$
