# AMC12 2004-B Q01

![[assets/problems/2004-B/Q01.png]]

## Problem

At each basketball practice last week, Jenny made twice as many free throws as she made at the previous practice. At her fifth practice she made $48$ free throws. How many free throws did she make at the first practice?

(A) $3$
(B) $6$
(C) $9$
(D) $12$
(E) $15$

## Answer

**A.** $3$

## Solution

Each practice she made twice the previous, so going backward we halve. From the fifth practice at $48,$ the earlier practices had $24,$ $12,$ $6,$ and $3$ free throws.

Thus, the correct answer is **A**.

## Knowledge points

- Geometric sequence
- Work backwards

## Key idea

Use the common ratio and work backwards from the final term.

## Common mistakes

- Do not use a formula before identifying exactly which quantities the statement fixes.
- Keep track of units, signs, and endpoint conditions throughout the calculation.
