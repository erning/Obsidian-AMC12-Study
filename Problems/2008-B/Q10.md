# AMC12 2008-B Q10

![[assets/problems/2008-B/Q10.png]]

## Problem

Bricklayer Brenda would take $9$ hours to build a chimney alone, and bricklayer Brandon would take $10$ hours to build it alone. When they work together, they talk a lot, and their combined output is decreased by $10$ bricks per hour. Working together, they build the chimney in $5$ hours. How many bricks are in the chimney?

(A) $500$
(B) $900$
(C) $950$
(D) $1000$
(E) $1900$

## Answer

**B.** $900$

## Solution

Let $n$ be the number of bricks. Alone, Brenda lays $\tfrac{n}{9}$ bricks per hour and Brandon lays $\tfrac{n}{10}.$ Together, their rate is $\tfrac{n}{9} + \tfrac{n}{10} - 10.$

Working for $5$ hours completes the chimney:
$$
 5\left(\tfrac{n}{9} + \tfrac{n}{10} - 10\right) = n.
$$
 Expanding, $\tfrac{5n}{9} + \tfrac{5n}{10} - 50 = n,$ so $\tfrac{95n}{90} - n = 50,$ giving $\tfrac{5n}{90} = 50.$

Hence $n = 900.$

Thus, the correct answer is **B**.

- Rate
- Linear equation

## Key idea

Write each worker or vehicle contribution as a rate and solve the resulting equation.
