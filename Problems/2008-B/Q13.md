# AMC12 2008-B Q13

![[assets/problems/2008-B/Q13.png]]

## Problem

Vertex $E$ of equilateral $\triangle ABE$ is in the interior of unit square $ABCD.$ Let $R$ be the region consisting of all points inside $ABCD$ and outside $\triangle ABE$ whose distance from $\overline{AD}$ is between $\tfrac{1}{3}$ and $\tfrac{2}{3}.$ What is the area of $R?$

(A) $\dfrac{12 - 5\sqrt{3}}{72}$
(B) $\dfrac{12 - 5\sqrt{3}}{36}$
(C) $\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{18}$
(D) $\dfrac{3 - \sqrt{3}}{9}$
(E) $\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{12}$

## Answer

**B.** $\dfrac{12 - 5\sqrt{3}}{36}$

## Solution

![[assets/problems/2008-B/Q13-draft.svg]]

Place $A = (0,0),$ $B = (1,0),$ $C = (1,1),$ $D = (0,1),$ so $\overline{AD}$ lies along the $y$-axis and distance from $\overline{AD}$ is the $x$-coordinate. The region lies in the strip $\tfrac13 \le x \le \tfrac23,$ which within the square has area $\tfrac13.$

Equilateral $\triangle ABE$ has $E = \left(\tfrac12, \tfrac{\sqrt3}{2}\right),$ with side $AE$ on $y = \sqrt3\,x$ and side $BE$ on $y = \sqrt3(1 - x).$ The area of the triangle inside the strip is
$$
 \begin{aligned} &\int_{1/3}^{1/2} \sqrt3\,x\,dx \\ &\quad {}+ \int_{1/2}^{2/3} \sqrt3(1 - x)\,dx \\ &= 2\int_{1/3}^{1/2}\sqrt3\,x\,dx \\ &= \frac{5\sqrt3}{36}. \end{aligned}
$$

Therefore
$$
 [R] = \frac13 - \frac{5\sqrt3}{36} = \frac{12 - 5\sqrt3}{36}.
$$

Thus, the correct answer is **B**.
