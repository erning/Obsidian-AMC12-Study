# AMC12 2020-A Q07

![[assets/problems/2020-A/Q07.png]]

## Problem

Seven cubes, whose volumes are $1,8,27,64,125,216,$ and $343$ cubic units, are stacked vertically to form a tower in which the volumes decrease from bottom to top. Except for the bottom cube, the bottom face of each cube lies completely on top of the cube below it. What is the total surface area of the tower, including the bottom?

## Answer

**B.** $658$

## Solution

The cube side lengths are $1,2,\ldots,7$. The four vertical faces of cube $k$ contribute $4k^2$, so all vertical faces contribute

$$4\sum_{k=1}^7k^2=4\cdot140=560.$$

Because every upper cube lies completely on the cube below it, the upward-facing horizontal pieces have total projected area equal to the top face of the bottom cube, namely $7^2=49$. Looking from below gives another $49$ for the bottom-facing horizontal pieces. Therefore

$$560+49+49=658.$$

## Knowledge points

- Surface area of cubes
- Projection and non-overlapping stacked areas

## Key idea

Count vertical faces individually, then use projection to count all exposed horizontal pieces without tracking every overlap.

## Common mistakes

- Adding all six faces of every cube and double-counting hidden faces.
- Forgetting the bottom face of the tower.
