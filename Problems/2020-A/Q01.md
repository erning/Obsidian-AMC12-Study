# AMC12 2020-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2020-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Carlos took $70\%$ of a whole pie. Maria took one third of the remainder. What portion of the whole pie was left?

## Answer

**C.** $20\%$

## Solution

After Carlos takes $70\%$, the remaining portion is $30\%$. Maria takes one third of this remainder:

$$\frac13\cdot30\%=10\%.$$

Therefore the portion left is

$$30\%-10\%=20\%.$$

## Knowledge points

- Percentages
- Successive operations on a remainder

## Key idea

Maria's one third applies to the remaining $30\%$, not to the original pie.

## Common mistakes

- Subtracting one third of $100\%$ instead of one third of $30\%$.
- Reporting Maria's portion rather than the final remainder.
