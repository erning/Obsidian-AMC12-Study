# AMC12 2015-B Q02

![[assets/problems/2015-B/Q02.png]]

## Problem

Marie does three equally time-consuming tasks in a row without taking breaks. She begins the first task at 1:00 PM and finishes the second task at 2:40 PM. When does she finish the third task?

## Answer

**B.** 3:30 PM

## Solution

The first two tasks take $100$ minutes, from 1:00 PM to 2:40 PM. Since the tasks have equal lengths, each task takes

$$\frac{100}{2}=50\text{ minutes}.$$

The third task ends $50$ minutes after 2:40 PM, at 3:30 PM.

## Knowledge points

- Rates and time
- Converting hours to minutes

## Key idea

Use the elapsed time for two equal tasks to find the length of one task.

## Common mistakes

- Counting 1:00 PM to 2:40 PM as $1$ hour $40$ minutes without converting to $100$ minutes
- Adding $100$ minutes instead of one task length to 2:40 PM
