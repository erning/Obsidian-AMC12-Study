# AMC12 2015-B Q01

![[assets/problems/2015-B/Q01.png]]

## Problem

What is the value of $2-(-2)^{-2}$?

## Answer

**C.** $\frac{7}{4}$

## Solution

A negative exponent means a reciprocal:

$$(-2)^{-2}=\frac{1}{(-2)^2}=\frac14.$$

Therefore,

$$2-(-2)^{-2}=2-\frac14=\frac74.$$

## Knowledge points

- Negative exponents
- Order of operations

## Key idea

Evaluate the negative exponent before performing the subtraction.

## Common mistakes

- Treating $(-2)^{-2}$ as $-4$
- Forgetting that the even power makes $(-2)^2$ positive
