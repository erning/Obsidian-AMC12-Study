## Problem

What is $$(20-(2010-201))+(2010-(201-20))?$$

(A) $-4020$
(B) $0$
(C) $40$
(D) $401$
(E) $4020$

## Answer

**C.** $40$

## Solution

Distributing the negative signs gives $$ \begin{aligned} &(20-2010+201) \\ &\quad {}+(2010-201+20)=40. \end{aligned} $$

Thus, **C** is the correct answer.

## Knowledge points

- Whole number operations
- Order of operations

## Key idea

Evaluate the two parenthesized expressions carefully, respecting the order of subtraction before adding the terms.

## Common mistakes

- Applying a formula before identifying the relevant quantities and constraints.
- Counting invalid cases or overlooking a boundary condition from the statement.
