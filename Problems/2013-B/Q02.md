# AMC12 2013-B Q02

![[assets/problems/2013-B/Q02.png]]

## Problem

Mr. Green measures his rectangular garden by walking two sides and finds that it is $15$ steps by $20$ steps. Each step is $2$ feet long. He expects half a pound of potatoes per square foot. How many pounds of potatoes does he expect?

## Answer

**A.** $600$

## Solution

The garden dimensions are
$$15\cdot2=30\text{ ft},\qquad20\cdot2=40\text{ ft}.$$
Its area is $30\cdot40=1200$ square feet. At half a pound per square foot, the expected harvest is
$$\frac12\cdot1200=600\text{ pounds}.$$

## Knowledge points

- Unit conversion
- Rectangle area
- Rates

## Key idea

Convert both side lengths before calculating area.

## Common mistakes

- Converting only one side
- Applying the yield rate before finding the area
