# AMC12 2016-B Q07

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q07.png]]

## Problem

Josh repeatedly marks every other remaining number in $1,2,\ldots,100$, starting with the first. What number remains?

## Answer

**D.** $64$

## Solution

After the first pass, only multiples of $2$ remain; after the second, only multiples of $4$ remain. After each pass the surviving numbers are multiples of a higher power of $2$. The largest power of $2$ at most $100$ is
$$2^6=64.$$

## Knowledge points

- Iterative elimination
- Powers of two

## Key idea

Track the set of survivors after each complete pass.

## Common mistakes

- Tracking the marked numbers instead of survivors
- Stopping at $2^5=32$
