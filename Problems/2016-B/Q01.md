# AMC12 2016-B Q01

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Evaluate $\dfrac{2a^{-1}+\frac{a^{-1}}2}{a}$ when $a=\frac12$.

## Answer

**D.** $10$

## Solution

Since $a^{-1}=2$, the numerator is $2(2)+2/2=5$. Dividing by $a=1/2$ gives
$$\frac5{1/2}=10.$$

## Knowledge points

- Negative exponents
- Fraction simplification

## Key idea

Substitute $a=1/2$ and compute $a^{-1}$ first.

## Common mistakes

- Reading $a^{-1}$ as $-a$
- Forgetting the outer division by $a$
