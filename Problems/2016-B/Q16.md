# AMC12 2016-B Q16

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q16.png]]

## Problem

In how many ways can $345$ be written as a sum of at least two consecutive positive integers?

## Answer

**E.** $7$

## Solution

For odd-length runs, the middle term is a divisor of $345=3\cdot5\cdot23$, producing lengths $3,5,15,23$ and four representations. For even lengths, the half-integer midpoint condition produces lengths $2,6,10$, giving three more. Thus the total is $7$.

## Knowledge points

- Consecutive sums
- Divisors and parity

## Key idea

Separate odd and even numbers of terms.

## Common mistakes

- Counting the one-term representation
- Listing divisors without checking positivity
