# AMC12 2016-B Q05

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q05.png]]

## Problem

The War of 1812 began on Thursday, June 18, 1812. The peace treaty was signed 919 days later. What day was it?

## Answer

**B.** Saturday

## Solution

Since
$$919=7\cdot131+2,$$
only two weekdays matter. Two days after Thursday is Saturday.

## Knowledge points

- Modular arithmetic with weekdays
- Periodicity

## Key idea

Reduce the number of days modulo $7$.

## Common mistakes

- Counting the starting day as day $1$
- Using the wrong weekday shift
