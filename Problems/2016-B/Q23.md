# AMC12 2016-B Q23

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q23.png]]

## Problem

Find the volume satisfying $|x|+|y|+|z|\le1$ and $|x|+|y|+|z-1|\le1$.

## Answer

**A.** $\frac16$

## Solution

Each inequality describes a regular octahedron of volume $4/3$. The two octahedra are shifted by one unit in the $z$-direction. Their intersection is a scaled octahedron with all linear dimensions half as large, so its volume is
$$\left(\frac12\right)^3\frac43=\frac16.$$

## Knowledge points

- Three-dimensional geometry
- Volume scaling

## Key idea

Identify the two octahedra and analyze their intersection.

## Common mistakes

- Adding the two volumes
- Scaling volume linearly instead of cubically
