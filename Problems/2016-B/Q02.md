# AMC12 2016-B Q02

![[assets/problems/2016-B/Q02.png]]

## Problem

The harmonic mean of $1$ and $2016$ is closest to which integer?

## Answer

**A.** $2$

## Solution

The harmonic mean is
$$\frac{2\cdot1\cdot2016}{1+2016}=\frac{4032}{2017}=2-\frac2{2017},$$
which is just below $2$.

## Knowledge points

- Harmonic mean
- Estimation

## Key idea

Calculate the exact expression, then compare it with nearby integers.

## Common mistakes

- Using the arithmetic mean
- Rounding $4032/2017$ to $1$
