# AMC12 2024-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2024-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

What is the value of

$$
9901\cdot101-99\cdot10101?
$$

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(A) $2$**

## Solution

Rewrite each product so that a large cancellation appears:

$$
9901\cdot101=9901(100+1)=990100+9901=1{,}000{,}001,
$$

and

$$
99\cdot10101=10101(100-1)=1{,}010{,}100-10{,}101=999{,}999.
$$

Therefore

$$
1{,}000{,}001-999{,}999=\boxed2.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Distributive property
- Recognizing convenient decompositions

## Key idea

Rewrite $101$ as $100+1$ and $99$ as $100-1$.

## Common mistakes

- Multiplying the two products completely without looking for structure.
- Misplacing the zeros in $1{,}000{,}001$ and $999{,}999$.
