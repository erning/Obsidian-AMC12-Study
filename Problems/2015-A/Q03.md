# AMC12 2015-A Q03

![[assets/problems/2015-A/Q03.png]]

## Problem

The average of 14 tests is $80$, and after Payton's test the average of 15 tests is $81$. Find Payton's score.

## Answer

**E.** $95$

## Solution

The two totals are
$$14\cdot80=1120,\qquad15\cdot81=1215.$$
Payton's score is $1215-1120=95$.

## Knowledge points

- Averages as totals
- Linear subtraction

## Key idea

Compare the total before and after adding the one score.

## Common mistakes

- Averaging $80$ and $81$
- Using $15\cdot80$ for the first total
