# AMC12 2015-A Q18

![[assets/problems/2015-A/Q18.png]]

## Problem

The zeros of $f(x)=x^2-ax+2a$ are integers. Find the sum of all possible values of $a$.

## Answer

**C.** $16$

## Solution

Let integer roots be $p,q$. Vieta gives $p+q=a$ and $pq=2a$, so
$$(p-2)(q-2)=4.$$
The integer factor pairs yield $a\in\{-1,0,8,9\}$, whose sum is $16$.

## Knowledge points

- Vieta's formulas
- Integer factor pairs

## Key idea

Replace the coefficient condition with a factorization in the roots.

## Common mistakes

- Counting duplicate root pairs twice
- Restricting roots to positive integers
