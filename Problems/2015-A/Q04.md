# AMC12 2015-A Q04

![[assets/problems/2015-A/Q04.png]]

## Problem

The sum of two positive numbers is five times their difference. Find the ratio of larger to smaller.

## Answer

**B.** $\frac32$

## Solution

Let $x>y>0$. Then
$$x+y=5(x-y),$$
so $4x=6y$ and
$$\frac{x}{y}=\frac32.$$

## Knowledge points

- Ratios
- Linear equations

## Key idea

Name the larger and smaller numbers explicitly.

## Common mistakes

- Reversing the difference
- Reporting $y/x$
