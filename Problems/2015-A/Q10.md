# AMC12 2015-A Q10

![[assets/problems/2015-A/Q10.png]]

## Problem

Positive integers $x>y$ satisfy $x+y+xy=80$. Find $x$.

## Answer

**E.** $26$

## Solution

Add $1$ and factor:
$$(x+1)(y+1)=81=3^4.$$
Since $x>y>0$, the factors are $27$ and $3$. Thus $x+1=27$ and $x=26$.

## Knowledge points

- Factoring Diophantine equations
- Divisors

## Key idea

Turn the expression into a product with a fixed value.

## Common mistakes

- Using factors $81$ and $1$, which gives $y=0$
- Ignoring $x>y$
