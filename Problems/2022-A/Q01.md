# AMC12 2022-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2022-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

What is the value of
$$
3+\frac{1}{3+\frac{1}{3+\frac13}}?
$$

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(D) $\frac{109}{33}$**

## Solution

Simplify from the innermost fraction outward:
$$
3+\frac13=\frac{10}{3},
$$
so the next layer is
$$
3+\frac{1}{10/3}=3+\frac3{10}=\frac{33}{10}.
$$
Therefore the whole expression is
$$
3+\frac{1}{33/10}
=3+\frac{10}{33}
=\boxed{\frac{109}{33}}.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Nested fractions
- Fraction arithmetic

## Key idea

Evaluate a nested fraction from the deepest level outward.

## Common mistakes

- Taking the reciprocal of the wrong partial expression.
