# AMC12 2019-B Q07

![[assets/problems/2019-B/Q07.png]]

## Problem

What is the sum of all real $x$ for which the median of $4,6,8,17,x$ equals their mean?

## Answer

**A.** $-5$

## Solution

The mean is $(35+x)/5$. If $x\le6$, the median is 6, giving $x=-5$, which is consistent. If $6<x<8$, the median is $x$, giving $x=35/4$, not in that interval. If $x\ge8$, the median is 8, giving $x=5$, also inconsistent. Therefore the only value is $-5$.

## Knowledge points

- Mean and median
- Piecewise case analysis

## Key idea

The median depends on the interval containing $x$.

## Common mistakes

- Solving with one median value without checking its interval.
