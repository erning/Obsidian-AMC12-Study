# AMC12 2019-B Q11

![[assets/problems/2019-B/Q11.png]]

## Problem

How many unordered pairs of edges of a cube determine a plane?

## Answer

**D.** $42$

## Solution

Two cube edges determine a plane when they intersect or are parallel. The 12 edges form three direction classes of four parallel edges, contributing

$$3\binom42=18.$$

At each of the 8 vertices, three edges meet, contributing

$$8\binom32=24.$$

These pairs are disjoint, so the total is $18+24=42$.

## Knowledge points

- Spatial geometry
- Combinations

## Key idea

Classify coplanar pairs as parallel pairs or intersecting pairs.

## Common mistakes

- Counting all pairs of edges.
- Double-counting intersecting edges.
