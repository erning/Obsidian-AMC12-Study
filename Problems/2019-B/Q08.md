# AMC12 2019-B Q08

![[assets/problems/2019-B/Q08.png]]

## Problem

Let $f(x)=x^2(1-x)^2$. Find the alternating sum

$$f\left(\frac1{2019}\right)-f\left(\frac2{2019}\right)+\cdots+f\left(\frac{2017}{2019}\right)-f\left(\frac{2018}{2019}\right).$$

## Answer

**A.** $0$

## Solution

The symmetry $f(1-x)=f(x)$ gives

$$f\left(\frac{k}{2019}\right)=f\left(\frac{2019-k}{2019}\right).$$

The partner of the term indexed by $k$ is indexed by $2019-k$. Since 2019 is odd, their alternating signs are opposite. Every term cancels with its partner, so the sum is 0.

## Knowledge points

- Symmetry
- Alternating sums

## Key idea

Pair indices $k$ and $2019-k$.

## Common mistakes

- Pairing $k$ with $2018-k$.
- Assuming the function is odd rather than symmetric.
