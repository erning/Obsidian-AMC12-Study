# AMC12 2006-A Q04

![[assets/problems/2006-A/Q04.png]]

## Problem

A digital watch displays hours and minutes with AM and PM. What is the largest possible sum of the digits in the display?

(A) $17$
(B) $19$
(C) $21$
(D) $22$
(E) $23$

## Answer

**E.** $23$

## Solution

The two minutes digits sum to at most $5 + 9 = 14,$ at $59$ minutes past the hour. For the hour, a single digit $9$ gives digit sum $9,$ which beats any two-digit hour $(10, 11, 12$ give at most $1 + 2 = 3).$

The largest total is $14 + 9 = 23,$ occurring at $9\!:\!59.$

Thus, the correct answer is **E**.
