# AMC12 2003-A Q04

![[assets/problems/2003-A/Q04.png]]

## Problem

It takes Mary $30$ minutes to walk uphill $1$ km from her home to school, but it takes her only $10$ minutes to walk from school to home along the same route. What is her average speed, in km/hr, for the round trip?

(A) $3$
(B) $3.125$
(C) $3.5$
(D) $4$
(E) $4.5$

## Answer

**A.** $3$

## Solution

Mary walks a total of $2$ km in $30+10=40$ minutes, which is $\dfrac23$ hour.

Her average speed is $2 \div \dfrac23 = 3$ km/hr.

Thus, the correct answer is **A**.

## Key idea

- Average speed is total distance divided by total time
- The total time is $30+10=40$ minutes $=\dfrac23$ hour
