# AMC12 2016-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2016-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Evaluate $\dfrac{11!-10!}{9!}$.

## Answer

**B.** $100$

## Solution

Factor the numerator:
$$\frac{11!-10!}{9!}=\frac{10!(11-1)}{9!}=10\cdot10=100.$$

## Knowledge points

- Factorials
- Algebraic simplification

## Key idea

Factor out the smaller factorial before expanding.

## Common mistakes

- Expanding factorials completely
- Dividing by $9!$ too late
