# AMC12 2016-A Q04

![[assets/problems/2016-A/Q04.png]]

## Problem

The mean, median, and mode of $60,100,x,40,50,200,90$ are all $x$. Find $x$.

## Answer

**D.** $90$

## Solution

The mean equation gives
$$\frac{540+x}{7}=x,$$
so $x=90$. The sorted data are $40,50,60,90,90,100,200$, whose median and mode are also $90$.

## Knowledge points

- Mean, median, and mode
- Linear equations

## Key idea

Use the mean first, then verify the other two conditions.

## Common mistakes

- Using the median condition alone
- Forgetting that $x$ appears twice when $x=90$
