# AMC12 2016-A Q06

![[assets/problems/2016-A/Q06.png]]

## Problem

A triangular array has $2016$ coins, with rows $1,2,\ldots,N$. Find the digit sum of $N$.

## Answer

**D.** $9$

## Solution

The triangular number equation is
$$\frac{N(N+1)}2=2016,$$
so $N(N+1)=4032=63\cdot64$. Thus $N=63$, and its digit sum is $6+3=9$.

## Knowledge points

- Triangular numbers
- Factoring consecutive integers

## Key idea

Recognize $2016$ as a triangular number.

## Common mistakes

- Solving a quadratic without checking the positive integer
- Reporting $N$ instead of its digit sum
