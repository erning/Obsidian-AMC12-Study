# AMC12 2016-A Q10

![[assets/problems/2016-A/Q10.png]]

## Problem

Five friends occupy seats $1$ through $5$. Bea moves two seats right, Ceci one seat left, Dee and Edie switch, and Ada ends in an end seat. Find Ada's original seat.

## Answer

**B.** Seat $2$

## Solution

The total displacement is zero. Dee and Edie's swap contributes zero, while Bea and Ceci contribute $+2-1=+1$. Ada must therefore move $-1$. Since she ends in an end seat, she ends at seat $1$ and originally sat at seat $2$.

## Knowledge points

- Signed displacement
- Conservation of total position

## Key idea

Sum all movements, including Ada's unknown movement.

## Common mistakes

- Treating a swap as movement in only one direction
- Assuming Ada started at an end
