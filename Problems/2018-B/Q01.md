# AMC12 2018-B Q01

![[assets/problems/2018-B/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Kate bakes a $20$-inch by $18$-inch pan of cornbread and cuts it into $2$-inch by $2$-inch pieces. How many pieces does the pan contain?

## Answer

**A.** $90$

## Solution

The pan area is $20\cdot18=360$ square inches, and each piece has area $2\cdot2=4$. Therefore the number of pieces is

$$\frac{360}{4}=90.$$

## Knowledge points

- Area
- Unit conversion within a grid

## Key idea

Divide total area by the area of one piece.

## Common mistakes

- Dividing side lengths instead of areas.
