# AMC12 2018-B Q07

![[assets/problems/2018-B/Q07.png]]

## Problem

Evaluate the product

$$\log_3 7\cdot\log_5 9\cdot\log_7 11\cdots\log_{23}25\cdot\log_{25}27.$$

## Answer

**C.** $6$

## Solution

Separate the factors with odd and even numerators. The odd chain telescopes:

$$\log_3 7\cdot\log_7 11\cdots\log_{23}27=\log_3 27=3.$$

The even chain gives

$$\log_5 9\cdot\log_9 13\cdots\log_{21}25=\log_5 25=2.$$

Their product is $3\cdot2=6$.

## Knowledge points

- Change of base
- Telescoping products

## Key idea

Use $\log_a b\cdot\log_b c=\log_a c$.

## Common mistakes

- Pairing adjacent factors instead of separating the chains.
