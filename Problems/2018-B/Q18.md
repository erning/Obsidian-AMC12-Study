# AMC12 2018-B Q18

![[assets/problems/2018-B/Q18.png]]

## Problem

Define $f(1)=f(2)=1$ and

$$f(n)=f(n-1)-f(n-2)+n\qquad(n\ge3).$$

Find $f(2018)$.

## Answer

**B.** $2017$

## Solution

Apply the recurrence repeatedly:

$$f(n)=f(n-1)-f(n-2)+n=f(n-3)+3=f(n-6)+6.$$

Thus $f$ increases by 6 whenever the input increases by 6. Since $2018=2+6\cdot336$,

$$f(2018)=f(2)+6\cdot336=1+2016=2017.$$

## Knowledge points

- Recurrence relations
- Periodic reduction

## Key idea

Look for a fixed-step relation instead of expanding 2018 terms.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming $f(n)$ is simply $n$.
