# AMC12 2019-A Q02

![[assets/problems/2019-A/Q02.png]]

## Problem

Suppose $a$ is $150\%$ of $b$. What percent of $a$ is $3b$?

## Answer

**D.** $200\%$

## Solution

The condition gives $a=1.5b$. Thus

$$\frac{3b}{a}=\frac{3b}{1.5b}=2.$$

So $3b$ is $200\%$ of $a$.

## Knowledge points

- Percent notation
- Ratios and proportional reasoning

## Key idea

Translate the percent statement into an equation before forming the requested ratio.

## Common mistakes

- Interpreting $150\%$ as $0.15$ rather than $1.5$.
- Finding what percent $a$ is of $3b$, which reverses the requested ratio.
