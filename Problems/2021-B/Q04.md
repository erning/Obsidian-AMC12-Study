# AMC12 2021-B Q04

![[assets/problems/2021-B/Q04.png]]

## Problem

Ms. Blackwell gives an exam to two classes. The mean score of the morning class is $84$, and the afternoon class's mean score is $70$. The ratio of the number of morning students to afternoon students is $\frac34$. What is the mean score of all the students?

## Answer

**C.** $76$

## Solution

Take the class sizes to be $3k$ and $4k$. The combined mean is the weighted average

$$\frac{3k\cdot84+4k\cdot70}{3k+4k}=\frac{252+280}{7}=76.$$

## Knowledge points

- Weighted averages
- Ratios

## Key idea

Use the class-size ratio as the weights; the actual value of $k$ cancels.

## Common mistakes

- Averaging $84$ and $70$ equally.
- Reversing the $3:4$ ratio.
