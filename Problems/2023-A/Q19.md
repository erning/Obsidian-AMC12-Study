# AMC12 2023-A Q19

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q19.png]]

## Problem

What is the product of all solutions to
$$
\log_{7x}2023\cdot\log_{289x}2023=\log_{2023x}2023?
$$

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(C) $1$**

## Solution

Let
$$
t=\log_{2023}x,\quad a=\log_{2023}7,\quad b=\log_{2023}289.
$$
Since $2023=7\cdot289$, $a+b=1$. By change of base,
$$
\log_{7x}2023=\frac1{a+t},\quad
\log_{289x}2023=\frac1{b+t},\quad
\log_{2023x}2023=\frac1{1+t}.
$$
Hence
$$
(a+t)(b+t)=1+t.
$$
Using $a+b=1$ gives $t^2=1-ab$. The two roots in $t$ are opposites, so the corresponding $x$ values are reciprocal. Their product is $\boxed{1}$.

## Knowledge points

- Change of base
- Logarithmic domains
- Vieta's formulas

## Key idea

Use $2023=7\cdot289$ so the transformed constants add to $1$.

## Common mistakes

- Changing the bases without including the factor $x$.
