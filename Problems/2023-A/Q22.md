# AMC12 2023-A Q22

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q22.png]]

## Problem

Let $f$ be the unique function on the positive integers such that
$$
\sum_{d\mid n}d\,f\left(\frac nd\right)=1
$$
for all positive integers $n$. What is $f(2023)$?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(B) $96$**

## Solution

For $n=1$, $f(1)=1$. For a prime $p$,
$$
f(p)+pf(1)=1,
$$
so $f(p)=1-p$. For $p^2$,
$$
f(p^2)+pf(p)+p^2f(1)=1,
$$
which gives $f(p^2)=1-p$.

The defining relation is a Dirichlet convolution, so $f$ is multiplicative. Since
$$
2023=7\cdot17^2,
$$
we get
$$
f(2023)=f(7)f(17^2)=(1-7)(1-17)=\boxed{96}.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Divisor sums
- Multiplicative functions
- Prime powers

## Key idea

Compute the function on prime powers and use multiplicativity.

## Common mistakes

- Replacing $f(n/d)$ by $f(d)$ without reindexing.
