# AMC12 2023-A Q14

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q14.png]]

## Problem

How many complex numbers $z$ satisfy $z^5=\overline z$?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(E) $7$**

## Solution

Taking absolute values gives
$$
|z|^5=|z|,
$$
so $|z|=0$ or $|z|=1$. The first case gives $z=0$. In the second case, multiply by $z$:
$$
z^6=z\overline z=1.
$$
There are six sixth roots of unity, so the total is
$$
1+6=\boxed{7}.
$$

## Key idea

Separate the zero solution from the unit-circle solutions.

## Common mistakes

- Counting only the six roots of unity.
