# AMC12 2023-A Q07

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q07.png]]

## Problem

Dates in 2023 are written as the eight-digit string $2023MMDD$, including leading zeroes. How many dates have every digit appearing an even number of times?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(E) $9$**

## Solution

The year contributes digits $2,0,2,3$. Thus the month-day portion must contribute odd parity for $0$ and $3$, and even parity for every other digit. Checking valid dates gives
$$
01\text{-}13,\ 01\text{-}31,\ 02\text{-}23,\ 03\text{-}11,\ 03\text{-}22,
$$
$$
10\text{-}13,\ 10\text{-}31,\ 11\text{-}03,\ 11\text{-}30.
$$
There are $\boxed{9}$ dates.

## Key idea

Track the parity needed to cancel the year digits.
