# AMC12 2023-A Q03

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q03.png]]

## Problem

How many positive perfect squares less than $2023$ are divisible by $5$?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(A) $8$**

## Solution

If a square is divisible by $5$, its square root is divisible by $5$. Since
$$
44^2=1936<2023<2025=45^2,
$$
the possible roots are $5,10,\ldots,40$. There are
$$
\frac{40}{5}=\boxed{8}
$$
such roots.

## Knowledge points

- Perfect squares
- Divisibility

## Key idea

A prime dividing a square divides its square root.

## Common mistakes

- Including the root $45$, whose square is too large.
