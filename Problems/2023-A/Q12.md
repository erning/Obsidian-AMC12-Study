# AMC12 2023-A Q12

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q12.png]]

## Problem

Evaluate $2^3-1^3+4^3-3^3+\cdots+18^3-17^3$.

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(D) $3159$**

## Solution

Group consecutive terms:
$$
\sum_{k=1}^{9}\left((2k)^3-(2k-1)^3\right).
$$
Each summand equals
$$
(2k)^3-(2k-1)^3=12k^2-6k+1.
$$
Therefore
$$
12(285)-6(45)+9=\boxed{3159}.
$$

## Key idea

Pair each even cube with the preceding odd cube.
