# AMC12 2023-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Alicia and Beth live $45$ miles apart. Alicia travels from $A$ toward $B$ at $18$ miles per hour, while Beth travels from $B$ toward $A$ at $12$ miles per hour. How many miles from $A$ are they when they meet?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(E) $27$**

## Solution

Their closing speed is
$$
18+12=30\text{ miles per hour}.
$$
The meeting time is $\frac{45}{30}=\frac32$ hours. Alicia travels
$$
18\cdot\frac32=\boxed{27}
$$
miles from $A$.

## Knowledge points

- Rates
- Relative motion

## Key idea

Use the sum of the speeds because the travelers move toward each other.

## Common mistakes

- Using the difference of the two speeds.
