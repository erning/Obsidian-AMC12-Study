# AMC12 2023-A Q10

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q10.png]]

## Problem

Positive real numbers $x$ and $y$ satisfy $y^3=x^2$ and $(y-x)^2=4y^2$. What is $x+y$?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(D) $36$**

## Solution

Since $x,y>0$,
$$
|y-x|=2y.
$$
The choice $y-x=2y$ gives $x=-y$, impossible. Hence
$$
y-x=-2y,\qquad x=3y.
$$
Substituting into $y^3=x^2$ gives
$$
y^3=9y^2.
$$
Thus $y=9$, $x=27$, and
$$
x+y=\boxed{36}.
$$
