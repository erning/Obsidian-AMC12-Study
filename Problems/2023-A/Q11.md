# AMC12 2023-A Q11

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q11.png]]

## Problem

What is the acute angle between the lines with slopes $2$ and $\frac13$?

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(C) $45^\circ$**

## Solution

![[assets/problems/2023-A/Q11-draft.svg]]

For slopes $m_1,m_2$,
$$
\tan\theta=\left|\frac{m_2-m_1}{1+m_1m_2}\right|.
$$
With $m_1=2$ and $m_2=\frac13$,
$$
\tan\theta
=\left|\frac{\frac13-2}{1+\frac23}\right|=1.
$$
Thus the acute angle is $\boxed{45^\circ}$.

Apply the slope-angle formula and choose the acute angle.
