# AMC12 2025-B Q02

![[assets/problems/2025-B/Q02.png]]

## Problem

Jerry writes down the ones digit of each of the first 2025 positive squares. What is the sum of all the numbers Jerry writes down?

**Choices:** (A) 9025 &nbsp;&nbsp; (B) 9070 &nbsp;&nbsp; (C) 9090 &nbsp;&nbsp; (D) 9115 &nbsp;&nbsp; (E) 9160

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(D) 9115**

## Solution

The ones digits of consecutive squares repeat every 10 terms:

$$
1,4,9,6,5,6,9,4,1,0.
$$

The sum of one complete block is

$$
1+4+9+6+5+6+9+4+1+0=45.
$$

There are

$$
2025=202\cdot10+5
$$

terms. The first 2020 terms contribute $202\cdot45$, and the remaining five digits are $1,4,9,6,5$, whose sum is 25. Thus

$$
202\cdot45+25=9090+25=\boxed{9115}.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Periodic patterns
- Units digits of powers
- Block summation

## Key idea

Find the period of the ones-digit sequence, sum one period, then handle the remainder.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming the pattern has period 4 instead of 10.
- Forgetting that 2025 leaves a remainder of 5.
- Summing the square values rather than their ones digits.
