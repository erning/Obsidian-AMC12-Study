# AMC12 2012-A Q02

![[assets/problems/2012-A/Q02.png]]

## Problem

Cagney can frost a cupcake every $20$ seconds and Lacey can frost one every $30$ seconds. Working together, how many cupcakes can they frost in $5$ minutes?

## Answer

**D.** $25$

## Solution

Five minutes is $300$ seconds. Cagney frosts
$$\frac{300}{20}=15$$
cupcakes, while Lacey frosts
$$\frac{300}{30}=10.$$
Together they frost
$$15+10=25$$
cupcakes.

## Knowledge points

- Rates
- Unit conversion

## Key idea

Convert the available time to seconds before dividing by each individual rate.

## Common mistakes

- Using $5$ instead of $300$ in the rate calculation
- Combining the times per cupcake instead of the rates
