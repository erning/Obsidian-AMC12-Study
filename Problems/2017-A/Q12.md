# AMC12 2017-A Q12

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q12.png]]

## Problem

Horse $k$ completes one lap in $k$ minutes. All start together. The least time for all 10 to return is 2520. What is the digit sum of the least positive time when at least five horses are at the start?

## Answer

**B.** $3$

## Solution

Horse $k$ is at the starting point exactly when $k\mid t$. We need the smallest $t$ divisible by at least five of $1,\ldots,10$. At $t=12$, the horses with indices $1,2,3,4,6$ are at the start. No smaller positive integer has five such divisors. Therefore $T=12$ and its digit sum is 3.

## Knowledge points

- Least common multiples
- Divisibility counting

## Key idea

Translate lap completion into divisibility.

## Common mistakes

- Looking for the least common multiple of five arbitrary horses without checking smaller times.
