# AMC12 2017-A Q02

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q02.png]]

## Problem

The sum of two nonzero real numbers is 4 times their product. What is the sum of their reciprocals?

## Answer

**C.** $4$

## Solution

Let the numbers be $x,y$. The condition is $x+y=4xy$. Dividing by the nonzero product $xy$ gives

$$\frac1x+\frac1y=\frac{x+y}{xy}=4.$$

## Knowledge points

- Algebraic manipulation
- Reciprocals

## Key idea

Divide the given relation by the product.

## Common mistakes

- Dividing without noticing that the numbers are nonzero.
