# AMC12 2017-A Q11

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q11.png]]

## Problem

The sum of the interior angles of a convex polygon was recorded as 2017 after one angle was omitted. What was the omitted angle?

## Answer

**D.** $143^\circ$

## Solution

If the polygon has $n$ sides and the omitted angle is $\alpha$, then

$$2017+\alpha=180(n-2),\qquad0<\alpha<180.$$

Thus the multiple of 180 lies strictly between 2017 and 2197. The only one is 2160, so $n=14$ and

$$\alpha=2160-2017=143^\circ.$$

## Knowledge points

- Polygon angle sums
- Integer bounds

## Key idea

Locate the only possible multiple of 180 in a short interval.

## Common mistakes

- Using $180n$ instead of $180(n-2)$.
