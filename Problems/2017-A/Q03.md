# AMC12 2017-A Q03

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q03.png]]

## Problem

Ms. Carroll promises that anyone who gets all multiple-choice questions right receives an A. Which statement necessarily follows?

## Answer

**B.** If Lewis did not receive an A, then he got at least one question wrong.

## Solution

The promise is the implication “all answers right $\Rightarrow$ A.” Its contrapositive is “not A $\Rightarrow$ not all answers right,” which means at least one answer was wrong. The converse and inverse are not logically guaranteed.

## Knowledge points

- Logic
- Contrapositives

## Key idea

Only an implication and its contrapositive are equivalent.

## Common mistakes

- Confusing the converse with the contrapositive.
