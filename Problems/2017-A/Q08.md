# AMC12 2017-A Q08

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q08.png]]

## Problem

The region within 3 units of a line segment $AB$ has volume $216\pi$. What is the length $AB$?

## Answer

**D.** $20$

## Solution

If $h=AB$, the region is a cylinder of radius 3 and height $h$, plus two hemispheres that form a sphere of radius 3. Thus

$$V=\pi(3^2)h+\frac43\pi(3^3)=9\pi h+36\pi.$$

Set this equal to $216\pi$ to obtain $h=20$.

## Knowledge points

- Volumes of solids
- Cylinder and sphere decomposition

## Key idea

Decompose the tube into a cylinder and a full sphere.

## Common mistakes

- Counting two hemispheres as two full spheres.
