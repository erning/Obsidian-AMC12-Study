# AMC12 2017-A Q07

![[assets/problems/2017-A/Q07.png]]

## Problem

Define $f(1)=2$, with $f(n)=f(n-1)+1$ for even $n$, and $f(n)=f(n-2)+2$ for odd $n>1$. Find $f(2017)$.

## Answer

**B.** $2018$

## Solution

The proposed formula $f(n)=n+1$ satisfies both rules. For even $n$, it gives $f(n)=(n-1+1)+1=n+1$; for odd $n$, it gives $f(n)=(n-2+1)+2=n+1$. Since the recursion is determined by $f(1)$, the formula is valid, so $f(2017)=2018$.

## Key idea

Recognize a simple formula satisfying both parity cases.

## Common mistakes

- Applying the even rule to an odd index.
