# AMC12 2023-B Q09

![[assets/problems/2023-B/Q09.png]]

## Problem

What is the area of the region in the coordinate plane defined by
$$
\bigl||x|-1\bigr|+\bigl||y|-1\bigr|\le1?
$$

## Answer

- Correct choice: **(B) $8$**

## Solution

In the first quadrant, the condition becomes
$$
|x-1|+|y-1|\le1.
$$
This is a diamond centered at $(1,1)$ whose diagonals both have length $2$, so its area is
$$
\frac{2\cdot2}{2}=2.
$$
Reflecting it into all four quadrants gives four congruent copies, so the total area is
$$
4\cdot2=\boxed{8}.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Absolute value
- Coordinate geometry
- Symmetry

## Key idea

Analyze one quadrant and use reflection symmetry.

## Common mistakes

- Treating the region as one diamond centered at the origin.
