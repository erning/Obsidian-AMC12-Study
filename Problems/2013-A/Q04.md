# AMC12 2013-A Q04

![[assets/problems/2013-A/Q04.png]]

## Problem

What is the value of
$$
\frac{2^{2014}+2^{2012}}{2^{2014}-2^{2012}}?
$$

## Answer

**C.** $\frac53$

## Solution

Factor $2^{2012}$ from the numerator and denominator:
$$
\frac{2^{2012}(2^2+1)}{2^{2012}(2^2-1)}
=\frac{4+1}{4-1}
=\frac53.
$$

## Knowledge points

- Exponent rules
- Factoring
- Rational simplification

## Key idea

Factor out the smaller power of $2$ so the large exponents disappear.

## Common mistakes

- Subtracting exponents across addition
- Expanding the powers instead of factoring
