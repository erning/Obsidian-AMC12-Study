# AMC12 2013-A Q01

![[assets/problems/2013-A/Q01.png]]

## Problem

Square $ABCD$ has side length $10$. Point $E$ is on $overline{BC}$, and the area of $	riangle ABE$ is $40$. What is $BE$?

## Answer

**E.** $8$

## Solution

The legs of right triangle $ABE$ are $AB=10$ and $BE$. Therefore,
$$
\frac12\cdot 10\cdot BE=40.
$$
Solving gives
$$BE=8.$$

## Knowledge points

- Triangle area
- Base and height

## Key idea

Use the side of the square as the height of triangle $ABE$.

## Common mistakes

- Using $BC$ instead of $BE$ as the triangle base
- Forgetting the factor $\frac12$ in the triangle area formula
